# Algorithmic_Trading
Built a daily backtest on SPTL (2023) and benchmarked excess returns vs. EFFR

Introduction:
This report compares the effectiveness of two algorith-
mic trading strategies using the SPDR® Portfolio Long
Term Treasury ETF (SPTL). The strategies are analyzed
against the U.S. monetary policy, represented by the Ef-
fective Federal Funds Rate (EFFR). This paper evalu-
ates the alpha-generating capabilities of leveraged trad-
ing strategies relative to a risk-free investment bench-
mark. The SPTL ETF tracks U.S. Treasury bonds with
maturities exceeding 10 years. The EFFR, provided by
the Federal Reserve Bank of New York, denotes the in-
terest rate at which financial institutions lend reserve
balances to each other overnight, serving as the risk-free
rate. The paper first prepares time series data for SPTL
and EFFR, then designs two trading strategies, evalu-
ates their performance using key indicators, and draws a 
conclusion based on the results and discussion.
